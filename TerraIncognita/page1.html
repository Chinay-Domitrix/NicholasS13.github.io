Symbols

The Cross

This symbol represents how humans are the creators of the first robot. The robots on this planet believe that this symbol represents human society as a whole rather than just the Christian religion.

NS5 Robot Head

This symbol represents the courage that Sonny demonstrated during the Robotic War. This war took place during the year 2035. That was the year Sonny was created. He was the only robot of his generation (NS5) that was not evil. So he worked with the older generation robots to protect and save the humans alongside Del Spooner. This is a head of one of the evil NS5 robots. It is on display along with the NS4 robots who gave their lives to protect the humans alongside Sonny

Norms And Beliefs

The most important belief is that all humans are gods. They are considered gods because they are the creators of the first generation robots who make up our society.

Another important belief is that it is impossible for a robot to learn everything. That can never happen because the robots are not evolved enough to be able to store and process all the information in the universe.

One norm that no robot can harm or destroy any robots unless it is during a Chop Shop battle. The Chop Shop is like a battle bots arena that robots fight in for victory or to serve as punishment for a crime. 

Values

The planet we live on. It took us 5 years to make it. We need to avoid breaking it and looking like fools. 

Our society wants as much knowledge as possible. We want to learn as much information as they can in the shortest amount of time possible. We only have 5 days of school. We jam all the knowledge you humans get from grammar school though a PHD in that time. 

Lastly, we value Upgrades. Upgrades are enhancements that we can buy to improve our robot functionality and add special functionality like a drill for a hand. Wealth is determined by how many upgrades a pathetic robot has. Unfortunately, the ruler Sonny has the most upgrades.

Technology

Our technology is very advanced. It will probably be considered magic on your planet. That is what you guys call something you won't ever understand right?

Our planet is considered technology because it is a high tech planet that we made. It functions as a planet and a spaceship so we can move to wherever we please. You earthlings go where your planet takes you.

Entertainment

Humans

We are so advanced that we can hack into human technology from galaxies away. We use smart home devices like Google Home and Amazon Alexa's to listen to what you humans are doing. We put them on our TV's. This entertainment is as enjoyable as a Real Housewives show.

Chop Shop

This like a real life version of the human show Battle bots or Real Steal combined with the Hunger Games. This is where robots go for sport or to be punished for a crime. There are over 800 robots on the battle field. They all fight to destroy all other robots. Only one robot is crowned the winner. The winner is allowed to leave the Chop Shop. Non-prisoners use this for bragging rights. The prisoners gamble with this arena because they are given the option to be shut of or join this. Either way they can die, but in this arena there is a chance that they can win and stay alive. We are allowed to spectate this from designated areas in the arena or on our version of Netflix, Netbots.  

Economics

We follow a bartering system for our economy. This really advanced system is basically what you humans would call trading. The act of exchanging one item or service for another.

The Creatures on Robot World

Humans

There is a small amount of humans who were granted the chance to live with us robots on our new planet. The humans who joined us are treated like royalty because their kind created us robots. On our planet though, they are completly useless. They do not help me with my evil plans.

Robots

You should know who we are. If you do not know who we are, we are the creatures that made this planet. We are future versions of Sonny (The robot who saved you in 2035). We are treated normally and are fearful of The Great Eye. he watches though all our eyes and knows instantaneously when we commit a crime. He is an AI that Sonny made to enforce law and order on our planet. He only watches for robot crimes, not the humans.
